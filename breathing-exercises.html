Breathing Exercise Guide

Why Breathing Exercises Help

  • Activates the parasympathetic nervous system
  • Reduces cortisol (stress hormone) levels
  • Increases oxygen flow to the brain
  • Promotes relaxation and calm
  • Improves focus and concentration

Best Times to Practice

  • Morning: Start your day with calm energy
  • Before exams: Reduce anxiety and improve focus
  • Before sleep: Prepare your mind for rest
  • During stress: Immediate relief from overwhelming feelings
  • Any time: Regular practice builds resilience

Tips for Success

  • Find a quiet, comfortable space
  • Sit or lie in a relaxed position
  • Focus on your breathing, not perfection
  • Start with shorter sessions and build up
  • Be patient with yourself
